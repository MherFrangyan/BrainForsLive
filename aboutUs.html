Brain Fors Company was founded in 2016, in Gyumri, the second largest city of Armenia. When the company was founded, there were two main goals: base the company in Gyumri, thus enabling local talented professionals to find work in their hometown and deliver services of high quality. Brain Fors started with a team of 5 people, working hard and shifting the importance on providing services of high quality

During those years Brain Fors team has been and continues growing, being supplemented by new professionals that share the same vision with the company. Today, we are a rooted technology-intensive company that is proud to deliver a comprehensive suite of solutions comprising of web consultancy, on/off site services, custom software and web development, software and web application testing.

We work highlighting Your need and wishes and sharing Your vision. In each case, our development team put efforts to satisfy You fitting in time. We are responsible in any work we do and very attentive to smalest details. We value Your time and try to make the highest quality work done in the shortest period of time.
